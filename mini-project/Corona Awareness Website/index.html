Covid-19


Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first known case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide, leading to an ongoing pandemic. Symptoms of COVID-19 are variable, but often include fever, cough, headache, fatigue, breathing difficulties, and loss of smell and taste.

Vaccination is the KEY:

SIGNIFICANCE OF VACCINE: Expert doctors shared during the webinars that vaccines have created a revolution in the history of medical science. Through vaccine, a harmless substance from bacteria/virus is introduced in to human to create antibodies and immune cells which will fight against the bacteria or virus so that the body does not suffer the illness in the future. Vaccines are very important as they help in boosting the immunity of our body against the specific diseases. There has been a significant reduction in disease burden and mortality in diseases such as Polio, Pneumonia, Japanese encephalitis, Tetanus, diphtheria, Measles, Whooping cough etc. due to vaccination. In the context of Covid-19, Doctors emphasized that it is important for all to get vaccinated as until majority of the population is not vaccinated, we cannot contain the transmission of the diseases.
